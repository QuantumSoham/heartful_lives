Know Your Rights

At Heartful Lives, we are committed to empowering the transgender community in India with knowledge about their rights. Here are some key rights that transgender individuals should be aware of:

Right to Self-Identify Gender

Transgender people have the right to self-identify their gender, without any requirement for medical certification or sex reassignment surgery. Read more here.

Right to Equality

Transgender people are entitled to equality before the law and equal protection of their rights. Learn more here.

Right to Non-Discrimination

Transgender people cannot be discriminated against in areas such as education, healthcare, employment, housing, and access to public services. Find out more here.

Right to Vote

Transgender people have the right to vote and stand for elections in accordance with their self-identified gender. Read more here.
